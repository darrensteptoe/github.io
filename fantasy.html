League rules

FORMAT 10 teams. Redraft league with 4 keepers per team. Snake draft with randomized order. Half-PPR scoring. Season starts Week 1. Top 6 make playoffs (Weeks 15–17), one-week matchups each round. Matchup ties are broken by bench points.
ROSTER Starters: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 Flex (WR/RB/TE), 1 K, 1 DEF. Bench: 6. IR: 3.
TRANSACTIONS + WAIVERS Adds: max 5 per week, 35 per season. Trades: max 10 per season. Waiver period: 1 day. Waiver type: Waiver Budget (100). Players lock at game time.
TRADES Trades are reviewed/approved by commissioner. Trade deadline: November 21, 2025 (date updates each season). In-season draft pick trades are allowed. Any collusion results in removal.
KEEPERS Up to 4 keepers. Keepers lock on the date of the league draft.
CONDUCT Competitive lineups required weekly. No collusion, tanking, or roster abandonment. Trash talk is encouraged; disrespect/harassment is not tolerated.
