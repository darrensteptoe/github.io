League Rules

Short, enforceable rules. Designed for active, competitive managers.

League format

10 teams. Redraft league with 4 keepers per team. Snake draft with randomized order. Half-PPR scoring. Season starts Week 1. Top 6 make playoffs (Weeks 15‚Äì17), one-week matchups each round. Matchup ties are broken by bench points.

Roster

Starters: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 Flex (WR/RB/TE), 1 K, 1 DEF. Bench: 6. IR: 3.

Waivers + transactions

Waivers: 1 day. Waiver type: Waiver Budget (100). Players lock at game time.

Adds: max 5 per week, 35 per season. Trades: max 10 per season.

Trades

Trades are reviewed/approved by commissioner. Trade deadline: November 21, 2025 (updates each season). In-season draft pick trades are allowed. Any collusion results in removal.

Keepers

Up to 4 keepers. Keepers lock on the date of the league draft.

Conduct

Competitive lineups required weekly. No collusion, tanking, or roster abandonment. Trash talk is encouraged; disrespect/harassment is not tolerated.
